Frequently Asked Questions

To become a member, you can fill out the membership form on our website or visit our office. Membership is open to all women in the Jain community who share our values and vision. Please contact us for more details about membership benefits and fees.

We welcome volunteers for all our events! You can register as a volunteer by filling out the contact form above and selecting "Volunteer Opportunities" as the subject, or reach out to us directly via email or phone. We'll be happy to match you with opportunities that align with your interests and availability.

Many of our cultural and social events are open to the entire community, regardless of membership status. However, some programs may be exclusive to members. Please check the specific event details on our Events page or contact us for more information.

We greatly appreciate donations that help us continue our community service and cultural activities. You can contact us directly to discuss donation options, or send us a message through the contact form selecting "Donation Information" as your subject.
