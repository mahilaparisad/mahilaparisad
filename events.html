Religious

Mahavir Jayanti Celebration

9:00 AM - 6:00 PM Main Community Hall

Join us for a grand celebration honoring Lord Mahavir's birth anniversary. The day will include special prayers, pravachan (spiritual discourse), cultural performances, and a community feast. All are welcome to participate in this auspicious occasion.

Event Highlights:

  • Morning prayers and abhishek ceremony
  • Spiritual discourse by renowned Jain scholars
  • Cultural programs featuring traditional music and dance
  • Community lunch (pure vegetarian)
  • Children's activities and storytelling sessions
