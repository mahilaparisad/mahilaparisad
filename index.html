About Mahila Parishad

Mahila Parishad is the esteemed ladies' wing of Jain Samaj, dedicated to fostering cultural heritage, community service, and spiritual growth among women in our community.

Since our inception, we have been committed to organizing cultural events, religious gatherings, educational programs, and social welfare activities that strengthen the bonds within our community while preserving our rich Jain traditions.

Our organization serves as a platform for women to connect, learn, and contribute meaningfully to society while upholding the timeless values of Jainism - Ahimsa, Satya, Asteya, Brahmacharya, and Aparigraha.
