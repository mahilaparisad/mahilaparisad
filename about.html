About Us

Discover our journey, mission, and values

Our Story

Mahila Parishad stands as a beacon of cultural preservation, spiritual growth, and women's empowerment within the Jain Samaj community.

Founded with the vision of creating a dedicated platform for women to actively participate in cultural, religious, and social activities, Mahila Parishad has grown into a vibrant organization that touches the lives of countless families in our community.

Our organization was established with the understanding that women play a crucial role in preserving traditions, nurturing values, and building strong community bonds. Over the years, we have successfully organized numerous cultural events, religious celebrations, educational programs, and charitable initiatives that have strengthened our community fabric.

As the ladies' wing of Jain Samaj, we work in harmony with the broader community objectives while focusing specifically on creating opportunities for women's leadership, spiritual development, and social contribution.

Our Mission

To empower women in our community through cultural preservation, spiritual enlightenment, and social service while upholding the timeless principles of Jainism. We strive to create a nurturing environment where women can grow, lead, and make meaningful contributions to society.

Our Vision

To be a leading women's organization that exemplifies the values of Jainism while fostering a community of empowered, educated, and socially conscious women who actively contribute to the betterment of society and preservation of our rich cultural heritage.

Our Core Values

Guided by Jain principles, we uphold these fundamental values

Ahimsa (Non-Violence)

We practice compassion and non-violence in all our actions, fostering peace and harmony within our community and beyond.

Satya (Truth)

We uphold truthfulness and integrity in all our endeavors, building trust and transparency in our relationships.

Asteya (Non-Stealing)

We respect the rights and property of others, promoting fairness and honesty in all interactions.

Brahmacharya (Chastity)

We practice self-discipline and moderation, maintaining purity in thought, word, and deed.

Aparigraha (Non-Possession)

We embrace simplicity and detachment from material possessions, focusing on spiritual wealth and service.

Community Unity

We foster strong bonds within our community, supporting each other in times of joy and challenge.

Our Connection with Jain Samaj

As the ladies' wing of Jain Samaj, Mahila Parishad plays an integral role in supporting and advancing the broader community's objectives.

Jain Samaj serves as the umbrella organization that brings together various community groups, and Mahila Parishad specifically focuses on addressing the needs, aspirations, and contributions of women within this framework.

We work closely with Jain Samaj to organize major religious festivals, coordinate community service initiatives, and ensure that the voice and perspective of women are heard in all community decisions.

Our collaborative approach has resulted in numerous successful programs that benefit not just women, but entire families and the broader community, strengthening the Jain Samaj's mission of preserving our heritage and serving society.
